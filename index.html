How To CARE

Every Charitable Animal is a stuffed toy strategically selected to represent a community-based non-profit organization. Our hotel partners offer a Charitable Animal for sale to each hotel guest in their room or suite to help promote the selected non-profit organization partner. When the Charitable Animal departs in the company of the hotel guest, a charge is placed on the guest’s account representing the purchase. The hotel then remits a percentage of the proceeds from each sale to the non-profit organization represented by that toy. WHAT WE DO:

Charitable Animals facilitates focused fund raising opportunities for non-profit organizations by partnering them with dedicated, logically connected hotels and resorts. We provide all necessary research, contract negotiations and fulfillment services by:

Identifying partnerships between non-profit organizations and hotels (based upon location and local interest);
negotiating terms to the mutual benefit of all involved parties;
providing appropriate stuffed toys with unique messaging to hotels for sale to hotel guests; and
coordinating with hotels and non-profit organizations to allocate revenue and donations based upon sales.

Our Founders:

Charitable Animals is a Pennsylvania Limited Liability Company founded by Laurie Alberts Salita and Richard B. Salita. Their two young daughters, Hailey and Chandler, play an active role in the identification of non-profit organizations and the selection of representative stuffed toys.

Laurie Alberts Salita was born in Williamsport, Pennsylvania where she developed a deep-rooted appreciation for nature and a sense for the importance of community. She received her BA in communications and business from Villanova University and her JD from Villanova University School of Law. Laurie is an experienced attorney, a published author, a mentor in her field and the founder of a professional women’s organization dedicated to fostering and promoting the development of women in business. Laurie is also an accomplished equestrian. She has lived abroad, traveled extensively and loves the outdoors.

Richard B. Salita was born in the San Fernando Valley, California. He received his BA in history and education at UCLA while simultaneously owning and operating a successful food and beverage retail business in Brentwood. Rich moved to the Philadelphia area in 2001 and began a career in media sales for one of the largest national television networks. Rich has traveled all over the world, lived on both of the US coasts and operated richly diverse businesses. He is a uniquely talented and resourceful entrepreneur. Rich’s love for the water transcends the Pacific Ocean where he grew up. An experienced wake boarder and boater, Rich has navigated many of the country’s waterways and seeks opportunities to protect them. He is also an avid golfer and skier.

Laurie and Rich’s ideals, dreams and goals are grounded and supported by family, developed from their tremendously rich life experiences and fostered by their desire to help individuals, organizations and businesses alike connect in meaningful and mutually beneficial ways that have a positive impact upon the community.
